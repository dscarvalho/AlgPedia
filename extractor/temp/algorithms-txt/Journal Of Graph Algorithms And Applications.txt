ABOUT
The Journal of Graph Algorithms and Applications is an open access peer-reviewed scientific journal covering the subject of graph algorithms and graph drawing.[1][2] The journal was established in 1997 and the editor-in-chief is Giuseppe Liotta (University of Perugia).[3] It is abstracted and indexed by Scopus and MathSciNet.[4]
